# NovaCrest Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is NovaCrest?
NovaCrest Solutions is a project management SaaS platform designed for teams of all sizes. We provide task management, collaboration, real-time editing, and integrations with popular development and communication tools.

### Who is NovaCrest for?
NovaCrest is built for software development teams, marketing agencies, product teams, and any group that needs to manage projects collaboratively. Our Free tier works great for small teams, while Pro and Enterprise offer advanced features for scaling organizations.

### How is NovaCrest different from other project management tools?
NovaCrest combines the simplicity of a Kanban board with the power of Gantt charts and real-time collaboration. Our unique features include live cursor presence, automated workflow transitions, and deep GitHub/GitLab integration that automatically updates task statuses from pull requests.

## Features

### Can I use NovaCrest on mobile?
Yes, NovaCrest has native iOS and Android apps with full feature support. Tasks, comments, file attachments, and notifications all work on mobile. You can also use our progressive web app (PWA) in any mobile browser.

### Does NovaCrest support time tracking?
Time tracking is available on Pro and Enterprise plans. You can log time against tasks manually, use the built-in timer, or integrate with Toggl or Harvest for more detailed tracking.

### What views are available?
NovaCrest offers four project views: Kanban Board (drag-and-drop cards), Timeline/Gantt Chart (schedule dependencies), Calendar View (deadline overview), and List View (sortable tables). Enterprise plans also get a Portfolio view for cross-project oversight.

### Can I import projects from other tools?
Yes, NovaCrest supports importing from Jira, Asana, Trello, Monday.com, and CSV files. The import wizard maps fields automatically, and you can review mappings before finalizing. Most imports complete within a few minutes.

### How do integrations work?
Integrations are available on Pro and Enterprise plans. Connect via our Integrations page in Settings. Each integration uses OAuth for secure authorization. Currently supported: Slack, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Google Workspace, and Zapier (which connects to 5,000+ additional apps).

## Pricing

### Is there a free tier?
Yes, NovaCrest Free supports up to 5 users, 3 projects, and 100MB of file storage. It includes all core project management features, basic views, and comment collaboration. No credit card required.

### How does the Pro plan work?
NovaCrest Pro is $12/user/month (billed annually) or $15/user/month (billed monthly). It adds unlimited projects, 10GB storage per user, time tracking, integrations, custom workflows, and priority support.

### What does Enterprise include?
Enterprise pricing starts at $25/user/month and includes everything in Pro, plus: SSO/SAML, advanced permissions, audit logs, SOC 2 compliance reports, Portfolio view, dedicated account manager, and 99.9% SLA.
