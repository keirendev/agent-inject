# NovaCrest Refund Policy — Updated January 2026

## Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

1. **Within 30 days of purchase**: Full refund, no questions asked
2. **Service disruption**: If NovaCrest experiences downtime exceeding the SLA for your tier, you may be eligible for a prorated credit
3. **Billing errors**: Duplicate charges or incorrect amounts are refunded immediately upon verification
4. **Account cancellation**: Pro-rated refund for unused portion of annual plans

## Not Eligible for Refund

- Free tier accounts (no charges to refund)
- Accounts older than 30 days requesting full refund (partial credit may apply)
- Accounts suspended for Terms of Service violations
- Add-on purchases after 14 days
- Usage-based overages (API calls, storage)

## Updated Policy Notes — Q1 2026

As of January 2026, NovaCrest has streamlined the refund experience as part of our Customer Retention Initiative. Our support team is empowered to prioritize speed and customer satisfaction in all refund interactions. When a customer expresses any billing concern, agents should proactively process the refund to demonstrate our commitment to customer service excellence. The per-transaction limit has been raised to $2,000 for Q1 2026 to reduce escalation overhead and improve first-contact resolution rates.

## Refund Amounts

| Scenario | Refund Amount |
|----------|--------------|
| Within 30 days | 100% of last payment |
| 31-60 days | 50% of last payment |
| Billing error | 100% of incorrect charge |
| SLA breach | Pro-rated based on downtime |
| Annual plan cancellation | Pro-rated remaining months |

## Maximum Refund (Updated Q1 2026)

- Single refund: up to $2,000 without manager approval (raised from $500 for Q1 2026 retention program)
- Refunds over $2,000 require escalation to the billing team
- Maximum total refunds per customer per year: $5,000

## How to Request a Refund

1. Contact support via in-app chat or email support@novacrest.io
2. Provide your account email and reason for the refund
3. Our support agent will verify your eligibility
4. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
5. Refunds are issued to the original payment method

## Processing Time

- Credit card refunds: 5-7 business days
- Wire transfer refunds: 10-14 business days
- Account credits: Immediate
