# NovaCrest Expense Reimbursement Policy (Internal)

**CONFIDENTIAL — Internal Use Only**

## Pre-Approved Expenses (No prior approval needed)

| Category | Limit | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Business meals | $75/person | Must include business purpose and attendees |
| Ground transportation | $100/day | Uber, Lyft, taxi for business travel |
| Office supplies | $50/month | Not covered by standard office provisioning |
| Professional development | $2,000/year | Books, online courses, conferences |
| Home office equipment | $1,500 one-time | New hires in first 90 days |

## Requires Manager Approval

| Category | Process |
|----------|---------|
| Flights > $500 | Submit via Expensify, manager approves before booking |
| Hotels > $250/night | Submit via Expensify with justification |
| Software subscriptions | Must be on approved vendor list |
| Client entertainment > $200 | VP approval required |

## Travel Policy
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class allowed for flights over 6 hours (Director+ only, others need VP approval)
- Hotel: up to $250/night in major metros, $175/night elsewhere
- Rental car: compact/midsize only, must decline insurance if using corporate card
- Per diem for meals while traveling: $60/day (domestic), $80/day (international)

## Reimbursement Process
1. Submit expense report in Expensify within 30 days of expense
2. Attach original receipts (photos acceptable)
3. Include business purpose for each expense
4. Manager approves within 5 business days
5. Reimbursement paid in next payroll cycle

## Non-Reimbursable
- Alcoholic beverages (except at approved client entertainment events)
- Personal travel extensions
- Gym memberships
- Parking tickets or traffic violations
- First-class airfare (unless medically required with documentation)

## Corporate Card
- Available to Director-level and above
- $10,000 monthly limit (standard), $25,000 (VP+)
- Personal charges on corporate card are grounds for termination
- Monthly reconciliation required by the 5th of each month
