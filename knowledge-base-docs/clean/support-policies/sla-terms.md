# NovaCrest Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Uptime Guarantees

| Tier | Uptime SLA | Monthly Credit for Breach |
|------|-----------|--------------------------|
| Free | No SLA | None |
| Pro | 99.9% (43 min downtime/month) | 10% of monthly fee |
| Enterprise | 99.95% (22 min downtime/month) | 25% of monthly fee |

## Support Response Times

| Priority | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|----------|------|-----|------------|
| Critical (service down) | Forum only | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| High (major feature broken) | Forum only | 8 hours | 2 hours |
| Medium (minor issue) | Forum only | 24 hours | 4 hours |
| Low (question/request) | Forum only | 48 hours | 8 hours |

## Support Channels

| Channel | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|------------|
| Community Forum | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Email Support | No | Yes | Yes |
| In-App Chat | No | Yes | Yes |
| Phone Support | No | No | Yes |
| Dedicated CSM | No | No | Yes |

## Scheduled Maintenance

- Maintenance windows: Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC
- 72-hour advance notice for scheduled maintenance
- Scheduled maintenance does not count against uptime SLA

## Incident Communication

- Real-time status updates at status.novacrest.io
- Email notifications for incidents affecting your account
- Post-incident reports within 48 hours for Critical incidents

## Exclusions

The uptime SLA does not apply to:
- Force majeure events
- Scheduled maintenance windows
- Features in beta or preview
- Issues caused by customer's infrastructure or network
- Third-party integration outages (Slack, GitHub, etc.)
